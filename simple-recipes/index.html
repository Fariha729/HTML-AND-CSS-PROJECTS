About Us

Simple Recipes is here to make everyday cooking easy, enjoyable, and delicious. We share approachable recipes for busy home cooks, from comforting family meals to quick desserts.

Each recipe includes clear preparation steps, helpful timing, and a level of difficulty so you can cook with confidence.

Featured Recipe

Deep Dish Skillet Pizza

Prep Time

30–40 minutes

Level

Medium

Make a warm, cheesy deep-dish pizza in one skillet. A crisp crust, savoury sauce, and your favourite toppings make this a simple meal for sharing.

How to Prepare

  1. Heat the oven and prepare the pizza dough in an oven-safe skillet.
  2. Add tomato sauce, cheese, and your favourite toppings.
  3. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Sausy Tortelloni Farm

Prep Time

20–30 minutes

Level

Easy

How to Prepare

  1. Cook the tortelloni in boiling water until tender.
  2. Warm your favourite pasta sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix the pasta with the sauce and serve with herbs or cheese.

Mediterranean Chicken Thighs

Prep Time

20–30 minutes

Level

Easy

How to Prepare

  1. Season the chicken thighs with herbs, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan until browned and fully cooked.
  3. Add tomatoes and olives, then simmer briefly before serving.

Crispy Pork Katsu and Rice

Prep Time

20–30 minutes

Level

Medium

How to Prepare

  1. Coat pork slices in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry until crisp, golden, and cooked through.
  3. Slice the pork and serve it with warm rice.

Cheddar Cheese Biscuits

Prep Time

40–50 minutes

Level

Medium

How to Prepare

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, butter, milk, and cheddar cheese.
  2. Shape the dough into small biscuits on a baking tray.
  3. Bake until the biscuits are golden and fluffy.

Baked Cod and Scalloped Potatoes

Prep Time

30–40 minutes

Level

Easy

How to Prepare

  1. Arrange the cod and sliced potatoes in a baking dish.
  2. Season with herbs and bake until the potatoes are tender.
  3. Check that the fish flakes easily with a fork before serving.

S’mores Cake

Prep Time

50–60 minutes

Level

Medium

How to Prepare

  1. Bake the chocolate cake layers and let them cool.
  2. Layer the cake with chocolate, marshmallow, and graham crumbs.
  3. Top with toasted marshmallows before serving.
